You keep moving Downstream

The bear has lost your immediate trail, but now the elements are your enemy. You are exhausted and dehydrated.

You are soaking wet. Hypothermia is setting in. You must decide:

  1. Build a Fire

    It will keep you warm but might draw the bear (or rescuers) to your exact spot.




  2. Keep Moving

    Stay active to keep your blood flowing until you find a road or shelter, but you risk collapsing from exhaustion.
